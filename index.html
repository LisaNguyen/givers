FEATURES

Giversal - Revolutionising the way people donate.

  • Newsfeed

    Real-time updates on the latest events and campaigns hosted by the charities you follow.

  • Discover Page

    Discover the charities that support causes you care about with a click of a button.

  • History & Progress

    Allows you to track what your donations have been used to achieve by each organisation.

  • Charities List

    Browse through a comprehensive list of charities that are campaigning for causes you care about.

  • Social

    See what causes your friends are passionate about and who the're donating to.

WHY CHOOSE US

Transparency

Quick updates which allows you to see exactly how each charity uses the donations you've made.

Engagement

Allows charities to interact directly with their sponsors via the newsfeed which allows the user to like and comment on each post.

Freedom to Choose

Removes the pressure of donating by allowing you to pick and choose the causes you'd like to contribute your donations to.
